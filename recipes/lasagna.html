Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a beautiful italian dish, a great way to incorporate many of our favorite ingredients such as cheese and meat with multiple layers of it too!

Ingredients

  • 350g lasagna pasta sheets
  • 1 tsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 450g beef mince
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp. dried oregano
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 900g ready-made pasta tomato sauce
  • 450g ricotta
  • 50g grated parmesan
  • 700g mozzarella, sliced
