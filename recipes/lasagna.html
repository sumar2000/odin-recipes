Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a beautiful italian dish, a great way to incorporate many of our favorite ingredients such as cheese and meat with multiple layers of it too!

Ingredients

  • 350g lasagna pasta sheets
  • 1 tsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 450g beef mince
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp. dried oregano
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 900g ready-made pasta tomato sauce
  • 450g ricotta
  • 50g grated parmesan
  • 700g mozzarella, sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190 degrees. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente, less than 2 minutes. Drain and drizzle a bit of olive oil to prevent the sheets from sticking together.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large pot over medium-high heat, heat oil. Cook beef until no longer pink, breaking up with a wooden spoon, then drain fat. Return beef to pan and add garlic and oregano and stir for one minute. Season with salt and pepper, then add tomato sauce and stir until warmed through
  3. COmbine ricota, 25g parmesan, and parsley in a large mixing bowl and season with salt and pepper. Set aside.
  4. In a large casserole, spread a thin layer of meat sauce, a single layer of lasagne pasta sheets, a layer of ricotta mixture, and a single layer of mozzarella, then repeat layers. Top last year of sheets with meat sauce, parmesan, and mozzarella.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 15 minutes, then increase temperature to 200 and bake uncovered for 18 to 20 minutes
  6. Garnish with parsley and serve
